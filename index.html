Common Questions

  • Are we taking on new patients?

    • Yes we currently have the availability to take on new patients.

  • Do Doctors Bulk Bill?

    • Due to cuts in federal funding, doctors are unable to provide a bulk billing service to all patients.

    Doctors do provide bulk billing to

    • Children under 16 years
    • Pensioners over 80 years of age
    • DVA gold card holders
    • Antenatal shared care
    • Listed health checks and care plans

    For everyone else payment is required on the day.

  • How do I claim my Medicare rebate?

    • We can offer your rebate back on the day if you have your savings card with you. Alternatively, if you have listed your banking details with Medicare we can arrange for your rebate to go back into your account directly from Medicare.

  • What if I have lost my Medicare card?

    • Reception team can look up and see if you have a current Medicare card number and give you the number to apply for a replacement card. You can still be seen as a patient, however if you card is expired you will be required to pay on the day.

  • How old do I have to be to have my own Medicare card?

    • You can apply for your own card as young as 14. Reception team can help with your enquiries.

  • Do doctors do home visits?

    • No

  • Do the Drs run on time?

    • Our Drs do their very best to run on time, however unfortunately there are emergencies and difficulties that require extra time. Reception team will do their best to keep you up to date with waiting times.

  • What is the cancellation policy?

    • A 2 hour cancellation time is required otherwise a missed appointment fee applies.

  • Is there close parking?

    • Yes, you will find parking out the back with access via Invermay Rd or Cadorna St. Including disabled parking.

  • How can I get there via public transport?

    • Metro buses run regularly from the city or joining suburbs, the closest stop from the City is Stop 9. For more information visit: https://www.metrotas.com.au/timetables/launceston/north/

  • Need a repeat on a prescription?

    • Drs discretion – conditions apply. These can be ordered via hot doc, over the phone to reception or via a general appointment. You will need to allow 3 working days. Costs apply.

  • Do you require a new ongoing referral?

    • Drs discretion – conditions apply. These can be ordered via hot doc, over the phone to reception or via a general appointment. You will need to allow 3 working days. Costs apply.
